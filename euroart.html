Welcome to your art exhibit assignment for today

Location: European Art

Your song choices have indicated that you want something that makes you find the joy and beauty in everything. The European Art Pavilion will provide you with a display that matches your feelings.

Dassin embodies the upbeat vibe that you may be feeling while Debussy and Tiersen represent the more calm side that complements that upbeat, joyous feeling. The pieces of the European Art form a fun complement of those two feelings. Monet's famous Water Lillies brings you the drama of a lone beautiful piece while some of the other pieces including Bonaparte's self-portrait brings some of the simplicity of joyfulness.

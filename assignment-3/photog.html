Welcome to your art exhibit assignment for today

Location: Photography Gallery

Your song choices have indicated that you want something that there is a sense of nostalgia you are feeling and a bitter-sweetness. The Photography Gallery will provide you with a display that matches your feelings.

Dassin, Debussy, and Iver all try to capture and hold on to some aspect of a memory or place whether that be the Paris streets, moonlit night, or a midwest highway, respectively. Photography attempts to do the same thing in it's framing, color grading, editing, and staging. The fight to hold on to a specific moment can be replicated both in the music choices as well as the photos displayed in this gallery.

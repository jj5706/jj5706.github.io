Welcome to your art exhibit assignment for today

Location: Asian Art Galleries

Your song choices have indicated that you want something quieter but still filled with big feelings. The East Asian Galleries will provide you with a display that matches your feelings.

The emotions of Debussy, Radiohead, and Farwell come alive through the variation of art pieces on display in the Huo Pavillion. Whether that be in the Tang dynasty scrolls, Liao dynasty panel, or a beautiful Chinese Buddhist sculpture, each of these pieces are dramatic but also a bit subdued, an oxymoron captured in the music you chose.

This exhibit also encourages you to slow down and enjoy the intricacies of each piece, matching the songs.

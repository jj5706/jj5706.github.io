Welcome to your art exhibit assignment for today

Location: John Wilmerding Gallery of American Art Art

Your song choices have indicated that you want something that feels alive but also a bit effortless. The American Art Gallery will provide you with a display that matches your feelings.

ELO's stacking orchestra on top of voices and harmonies along with JVKE's capture of a summer afternoon all give off the liveliness without it being overbearing. The American Art exhibit captures something similar in how it allows you to feel comfortable with familiar works but still make room for exploration through diverse pieces including pieces of Native Americans.

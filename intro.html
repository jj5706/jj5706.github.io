Which Exhibit Are You? Welcome to the song exhibit matching website for the Princeton Art Museum!

Have you ever wanted to wander the museum but don't know where to go? Do you like listening to music while in the museum? Do you enjoy solo viewing the museum?

If you answered yes to these questions, you have come to the right place! This website will give you several song options and allow you to choose between them. At the end of the website, you will be given an exhibit in the art museum that matches the vibes and feelings of the songs you chose.

There are no right or wrong answers - listen to the songs, take your time, and choose which one you are feeling most right now.

Stop 3

Location: Mosaic Cafe and Museum Exhibits

From the art museum, you will go inside the art museum, take a right, and walk straight up the stairs to the Monet exhibit on the second floor. Enjoy the Monet for a bit, stroll around the art museum and enjoy. You don't have any assignments so enjoy the art museum. After you have sufficiently explored the museum, you will go to the Mosaic restaurant in the art museum and pick up a pastry to go.

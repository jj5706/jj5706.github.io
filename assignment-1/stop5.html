Stop 5

Location: Coffee Club

From Murray Dodge, walk right and walk until you get to Washington Rd. Take a right and keep walking until you hit Prospect Ave. Take another right and go inside the first building (called Campus Club) From the lobby of Campus Club, go straight until you see the windows and go down the stairs, take a right and you are at coffee club. They have lots of delicious coffee options but you are loving the new Valentines day themed ones so you order the tiramisu latte with oat milk.

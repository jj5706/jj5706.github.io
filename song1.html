Welcome to the song quiz website for the Princeton Art Museum!

This website will allow you to choose between songs to determine the gallery that best fits your vibe for today. Among the many galleries at the museum, each carries a different vibe and your choice of song can determine which one to pick. There are no wrong answers so enjoy!
